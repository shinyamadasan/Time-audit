Welcome to ChronaSense
Most people think they know where their time goes.

They're wrong.

ChronaSense pings you every 30 minutes and asks one question: what were you just doing? Over time, the truth becomes impossible to ignore.
🔔
The core loop
1. Start the timer — tap the big button on Today tab.

2. Work normally — every 30 minutes (or your set interval) you get a ping.

3. Log it — type what you did and pick a category. Takes 5 seconds.

That's it. Do this for a week and patterns emerge.
🗂

Every block you log gets one label. Pick the closest fit — don't overthink it.

Auto-track phone & PC
ChronaSense can fill in gaps automatically:

Phone (Android) — detects when you open YouTube, Instagram, TikTok and other apps. Sessions are logged automatically. Grant permission when prompted.

PC (Browser) — install the Chronasense browser extension for Chrome/Edge. It silently logs which sites you're on and syncs to your account.

Auto-logged entries never overwrite your manual logs — they only fill unlogged gaps.
🔗
Home screen shortcuts
Add quick-start buttons to your home screen using these URL schemes:

chronasense://start?task=Work
Starts the timer with "Work" pre-filled

chronasense://quicklog?task=Gym&energy=invest
Instantly logs a past block without starting the timer

Use any app launcher (e.g. Tasker, KWGT, iOS Shortcuts) to create one-tap buttons.
⚙️
One thing before you start
Go to Settings and set your timezone — this makes sure your day starts and ends at the right time.

You can also adjust the ping interval (default is 30 min) and your deep work goal.

You can replay this guide anytime from Settings.
